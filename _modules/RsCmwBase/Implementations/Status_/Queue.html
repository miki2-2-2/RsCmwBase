		"""SCPI: STATus:QUEue[:NEXT] \n
		Snippet: value: NextStruct = driver.status.queue.get_next() \n
		Queries and at the same time deletes the oldest entry in the error queue. Operation is identical to that of SYSTem. The
		entry consists of an error number and a short description of the error. Positive error numbers are instrument-dependent.
		Negative error numbers are reserved by the SCPI standard. \n
